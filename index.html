Consulting Networking Reception

This event is a unique forum designed to forge connections between Johns Hopkins students who are interested in consulting. Undergraduates interested in learning more about the consulting career path are invited to attend and get candid advice from their peers in this informal, judgement-free setting. Upperclassmen with prior experience in the consulting industry or who have secured full-time offers or internships are also encouraged to attend. No experience is necessary, and students of all majors and graduation years are welcome to attend.

This event aims to spark lasting professional connections and provide valuable information to those seeking consulting careers. It will consist of a 15-minute discussion panel of graduating seniors who have secured positions in top consulting firms, followed by a 90-minute networking reception.
